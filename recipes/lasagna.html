Lasagna Recipe to make your Grand Ma Jealous

yummy lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground beef
  • 1/2 medium sliced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 ounces of tomato sauce
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 24 ounces of mozzarella cheese

Steps to follow

  1. Put Pasta Water on to boil
  2. Brown the Ground Beef
  3. Cook the onions
  4. Add tomatoe sauce in the onions
  5. Drain the Lasagna noodles
  6. Layer the lasagna at least 3 layers.
